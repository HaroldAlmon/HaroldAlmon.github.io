How do I stop using brute force?

I solved Problem 31 - Coins Sum without using brute force but that required a lot of additional thought to finally arrive at the solution.

I saw a brute force solution after I solved the problem and realized I could have saved hours of work by using that approach; however, the brute force solution takes minutes to execute while the faster solution takes less than a second. As stated by the site, all problems can be solved by a moderately powerful computer in less than a minute. If your brute force solution takes less than a second, pat yourself on the back and move on to the next problem.
